Class: Glyphset

Glyphset() → {Glyphset}

new Glyphset() → {Glyphset}

This is a container of Glyph and their graphical properties including transformations, colors, number of time steps, duration of animations and group name. Please note that all glyphs in the glyphset share the same geometry however they may have different transformations.
